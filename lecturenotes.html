Understanding Habits and the Mind

For every experience we have had, whether we remember it or not, our brains keep these experiences stored as subconscious memories. These memories influence our thoughts, behaviors, and responses.

Humans have two minds: the conscious mind and the subconscious mind. The conscious mind is the seat of goals, ambitions, and desires and controls approximately 5% of our day-to-day activities. The subconscious mind governs the majority of our habits and automatic behaviors.

Habits form when we repeat certain actions until they become automatic. It is not the conscious mind that creates habits, but the subconscious mind. Understanding this is key to mastering self-change and inner programming.
